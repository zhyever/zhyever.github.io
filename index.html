Biography

I'm a 3rd-year PhD student at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), advised by Prof. Peter Wonka.

I got my B.E. and M.S degrees in computer science at Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China.

My research was concentrating on 3D reconstruction and now are switching to other domains, like generative models, RL, VLM, etc.
